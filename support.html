Juke Support

Juke shows real-time crowding levels at UW-Madison facilities, voted on by students.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does voting work?

Tap any facility, then tap Quiet, Busy, or Packed to cast your vote. Your vote influences the displayed status for that location. You can vote once per facility every 30 minutes.

How long do statuses last?

Statuses automatically decay over time — from Packed to Busy to Quiet — and reset to No Reports after 90 minutes of no activity.

Why is a location showing Closed?

Some facilities have set hours and will show as Closed outside of those times.
