Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Juke ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the Juke app.

Information We Collect

Juke uses anonymous authentication. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information. You are assigned a random anonymous ID when you first open the app.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data including votes submitted, facilities viewed, and crash reports. This data is used solely to improve the app experience and cannot be traced back to any individual.

Third-Party Services

Juke uses Firebase (Google) for data storage and analytics. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Data Retention

Anonymous vote data is automatically deleted after 90 minutes as part of normal app function.

Children's Privacy

Juke is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
